Describe the room you're sitting in.

I'm in room 206 in the visual art building. I'm thinking about CSS media queries. The room has a lot of natural light from large windows on two sides. There are 7 windows, 3 on one side, 4 on another. There is a large table in the center, and everyone is on their computers and quietly typing while also looking around the room, sensing. The table is covered in some white paper. When I touch it, it makes a papery sound. One of the windows is slightly open because the room was too hot originally. Some wind is blowing through the opening, as I can see papers from previous art classes lightly moving. I heard this building used to be an elementary school, and after that, the English Department of Princeton. Someone told me that Toni Morrison once had an office in this building. The floors are gray. The sound of people typing relatively quietly suits the weather outside, lightly rainy and cloudy.
