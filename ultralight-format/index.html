How to Send a Virtual Postcard

Guide Examples Send Your Own

Most of us are familiar with the concept of a postcard. By default, they are a simple and lightweight way of staying in touch. Likely few of us send postcards physically (often), which is too bad because they are a beautiful concept.

But you can easily send postcards (virtually). All you have to do is:

  1. Choose a photo from your camera roll
  2. Type "a postcard" from (wherever you are)
  3. Write a paragraph or some text (that would theoretically fit on the back side of your photo) which paints some sort of picture of your experience.

That's it.

A lovely thing about this format is it's open! You can do it via text, email, or who knows!
